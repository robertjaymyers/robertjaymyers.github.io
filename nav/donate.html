Donate

If you'd like to support the apps that I'm making and sharing, you can donate through PayPal.

You can also support it by sharing it on social media, or recommending it to family and friends.

My mindset is to make and share apps here that people can find useful or enjoyable, without ever tying a mandatory price tag to it. I've benefited greatly over the years from others doing the same and I don't want what I offer to be something you lack access to, or feel compelled to obtain through unethical means, because you're struggling financially. I want you to do great things and live a great life.
