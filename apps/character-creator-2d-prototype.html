Character Creator 2D Prototype

Prototype 2D Character Creation tool, with functionality for Saving, Loading, and Exporting combinations of character parts.

Character Creation tools are something I love the creativity of, so I made this tool for fun and practice to see how far I could go in a 2D space and what that might look like.

It's designed to be more of a starting point than a finished tool. Something that can be expanded on or changed in the future, to extend how many different parts there are and accommodate the modification of them in the UI. As of writing this, it uses 4 different parts: Head, Chest, Bottom, and Feet, corresponding roughly to the head, the top half of the body, the bottom half of the body, and a rough expected range for boots or shoes.

Features

Be creative and design your own character by combining parts in the way you desire and then exporting the combination to an image of your character that you can share. (Parts are very limited due to this being a prototype and my not having much artistic skill, so, uh... be creative with less! There may be total possible combinations almost over 10...!)

How to Use

Parts are swapped by clicking the arrows in either direction. Right-click the screen to get access to saving, loading, and exporting options.

From the right-click menu, you can choose:

  • New Character: Resets the character back to the defaults.
  • Open Character: Opens a saved character.
  • Save Character: Saves the current combination of parts for loading and modifying or exporting later. It's recommended that you save these in the Saves folder, since they are just plaintext files, so they could get confused with other files if you saved them just anywhere.
  • Export Character: Allows you to save the current combination of parts as a PNG image that you can share or stare at.
